Code For Silicon

Welcome to Code for Silicon

We are an online project-based learning platform for aspiring software engineers. Our goal is for you to gain relevant knowledge and practice in software development by working through the recommended projects on the website, so that they you achieve your dream job working at a big tech company in Silicon Valley (or wherever you desire)!

Free Users

Free users get access to Code for Silicon's entire project library. Projects are recommended to you based on your current skillset and completed projects.

Read More

Premium Users

Premium users get access to 1-on-1 support with mentors from our roster of highly experienced software developpers. You will also get access to exclusive educational content designed to accelerate your projects.

Mentors
